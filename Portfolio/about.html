About Me

Hey there! I’m Fahmi D, a passionate front-end developer with a flair for creating stunning, interactive web experiences. I’m a dual student at Adama Science and Technology University, pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). I’m also a bit of a tech enthusiast, always exploring new tools and frameworks to push the boundaries of what’s possible.

When I’m not coding, you might catch me tinkering with hardware projects, diving into a sci-fi novel, or sketching out my next big idea. I’m all about blending creativity with technology, and I bring a cool, unique vibe to every project I touch.
